About Max

Max Verstappen is a Dutch Formula 1 driver racing for Red Bull Racing. Known for his aggressive driving style and incredible racecraft, he became one of the youngest drivers to enter F1.

Career Highlights

  • Multiple Formula 1 World Championships
  • Youngest F1 race winner in history
  • Drives car number 1 for Red Bull Racing
